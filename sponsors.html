Sponsorship Opportunities

The IEEE Hyderabad Section Student Congress (SSC) is the flagship platform connecting students, educators, and professionals. By partnering with SSC, sponsors enable hands-on learning, leadership development, and meaningful engagement across the section. We invite contributions across the following categories:

1. Fund Sponsorship

  • Supports event logistics, venue setup, delegate kits, and essential materials.
  • Covers speaker travel, accommodation, and hospitality arrangements.
  • Contribution tiers: Platinum ₹1,00,000+, Gold ₹75,000+, Silver ₹50,000+, Bronze ₹25,000+

2. Food Sponsorship

  • Snacks and refreshments for participants, guests, and volunteers.
  • Special hospitality arrangements for keynote speakers and dignitaries.

3. Goodies Sponsorship

  • Event merchandise such as T‑shirts, eco-friendly kits, stationery, or souvenirs.
  • Customized IEEE-branded giveaways to enhance the participant experience.

4. Technical Sponsorship

  • Support for technical exhibits, demos, or training sessions.
  • In-kind contributions from technology partners to enhance the event experience.

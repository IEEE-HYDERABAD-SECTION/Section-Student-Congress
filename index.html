About IEEE Hyderabad Section SAC

IEEE Hyderabad Section Student Activities Committee is a vibrant community of student leaders, mentors, and volunteers representing institutes across Telangana and Andhra Pradesh. We collaborate to amplify the impact of IEEE initiatives, champion innovation, and create meaningful opportunities for every student branch in the section.

Guided by senior volunteers from industry and academia, the committee curates knowledge-sharing platforms, mentorship programs, and flagship events that help students explore emerging technologies and develop leadership skills. Together, we nurture a culture of service, professionalism, and lifelong learning within the IEEE Hyderabad Section.

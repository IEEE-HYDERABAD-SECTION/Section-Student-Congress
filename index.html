About the Event

A Flagship Student Congress

The IEEE Hyderabad Section Student Congress (SSC) serves as a flagship platform that unites students, educators, and professionals to exchange ideas, celebrate innovation, and foster collaboration. Through a blend of technical, professional, and leadership initiatives, SSC aims to inspire the next generation of engineers and innovators.
